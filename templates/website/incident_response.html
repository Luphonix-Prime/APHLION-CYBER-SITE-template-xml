What is Incident Response?

An Incident Response Service is a specialized service that helps organizations detect, respond to, and recover from cybersecurity incidents. It involves a structured approach to managing and mitigating the impact of security breaches, such as data breaches, ransomware attacks, phishing scams, or malware infections.

PROVEN METHODOLOGY

Our Proven IR Process

Following industry-standard NIST and SANS frameworks, our comprehensive 7-step incident response process ensures systematic and effective handling of security incidents.

NIST Compliant SANS Framework ISO 27035
STEP 1

Preparation

Develop comprehensive incident response plans, define roles and responsibilities, establish communication channels, and conduct regular training exercises.

STEP 2

Detection & Identification

Monitor systems for suspicious activity, analyze security alerts, and investigate reports to identify potential security incidents.

STEP 3

Containment

Isolate affected systems, shut down compromised services, and block malicious activity to prevent further damage.

STEP 4

Eradication

Remove malware, close vulnerabilities, and patch systems to eliminate the root cause and prevent recurrence.

STEP 5

Recovery

Restore systems from backups, reconfigure security measures, and ensure normal operations resume safely.

STEP 6

Post-Incident Analysis

Conduct thorough analysis, review forensic data, and identify lessons learned to improve security posture.

STEP 7

Communication

Maintain clear stakeholder communication throughout the process and document policy changes for future incidents.
